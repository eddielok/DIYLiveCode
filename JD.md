# Lead Platform Engineer

**LightWork AI** · London or Remote · Full-time, Permanent · Competitive Salary and Equity

---

## About LightWork

LightWork is building the AI system of action for UK lettings and estate agencies. We sit on top of the agent's CRM and run the operational work — prospecting, viewings, maintenance, compliance, arrears. A lot of that work runs through **Felicity**, our voice agent, who answers the phone when the branch can't.

---

## The Role

We're looking for a **Lead Platform Engineer** to work under our Head of Engineering and own the build across our stack. You'll be a great engineer first: the person who builds the hardest things and sets the technical standard.

Agentic AI runs through the heart of LightWork. We run an agentic engineering environment — developers work alongside Claude Code and AI agents every day, and the product itself is built on agentic pipelines. We expect you to be ahead of the team on this, setting the bar for how we build with AI and constantly raising the standards of quality and feature set.

---

## What You'll Do

- **Architect, design and build** scalable backend systems and APIs across our stack, end to end, from architecture through to production.
- **Design and maintain** microservice and event-driven architectures that support Felicity's real-time operational workflows.
- **Lead a small team of engineers** — drive their delivery, review their work, and be accountable for what ships.
- **Set the standard** for how we build with agentic AI, from Claude Code workflows to the patterns the whole team adopts.
- **Contribute to technical strategy** and architecture decisions, working directly with the Head of Engineering on what we build, how we build it, and what we don't build yet.
- **Maintain** secure, performant and maintainable systems, and reliable data pipelines as we scale across agencies.
- **Get close to the product** — understand the lettings workflows Felicity automates, so the systems you build solve the right problems.

---

## Who You Are

- **5–8+ years** of hands-on software engineering experience, ideally in AI, SaaS, or automation platforms.
- A **first-class or strong degree** in Computer Science, Engineering, Mathematics or a related field.
- Already a **lead engineer**, or very close to it — you've owned the technical direction of real projects and you're ready to own more.
- **Deep expertise in Node.js and NestJS.** Strong experience designing and building RESTful APIs and microservice-based systems. Ideally experience on frontend and Next.js too.
- A **real focus on agentic AI** — you've built with LLMs and agents, you understand what breaks in agentic pipelines at scale, and you use Claude Code and agentic workflows as a matter of course, not as novelties.
- You come from a **startup** (or as a standout performer at a top firm) — you've worked in a small, fast-paced team where you had to make the right call without a committee, and you performed.
- **Hungry.** You'd rather have ownership over a hard problem and a small team than a comfortable seat in a big org.
- You can **lead engineers and bring them with you** (in-person and remote), while still being the strongest builder in the room.
